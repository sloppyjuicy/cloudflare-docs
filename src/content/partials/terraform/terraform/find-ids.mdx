The Terraform configurations provided in this page need the zone ID (or account ID) of the zone/account where you will deploy rulesets.

* To retrieve the list of accounts you have access to, including their IDs, use the [List accounts](/api/resources/accounts/methods/list/) operation.
* To retrieve the list of zones you have access to, including their IDs, use the [List zones](/api/resources/zones/methods/list/) operation.
